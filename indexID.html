Crumbed Rubber

Crumb rubber is a material produced by shredding and commutating used tires. During the recycling process, steel and tire cord are removed, leaving tire rubber with a granular consistency.

Pyrolysis Oil

Sometimes also known as biocrude or bio-oil, is a synthetic fuel under investigation as substitute for petroleum. It is obtained by heating dried biomass without oxygen in a reactor at a temperature of about 500 Â°C with subsequent cooling.

Coal Substitutes

Coal Substitute or carbon black is a powdered form of elemental carbon in the form of near-spherical colloidal particles and coalesced particle aggregates of colloidal size, obtained by partial combustion or thermal decomposition of hydrocarbons.

Bio Diesel

Biodiesel is a liquid biofuel obtained by chemical processes from vegetable oils or animal fats and an alcohol that can be used in diesel engines, alone or blended with diesel oil.

Mission

The Siklus aims to be a cost effective, sustainable raw materials supplier for construction, manufacturing and energy industries. Ensuring the sustainability of industry, environment and an improved future for Southeast Asia

Attitude

We believe in creating innovative business models that deliver sustainability, quality and respect for the environment and its resources. We follow the principles of the circular economy in order to conserve natural resources and better utilise those that are already available. It is vital that economic growth works hand-in-hand with environmental conservation and sustainability, with business focusing to implement new innovative practises.
